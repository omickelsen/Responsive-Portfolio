About Me

Majestic, Mighty, Intelligent, Fierce, and Leader. These are many of the characteristics that I contain. I'm 26 years old and I am married to the love of my life Cassidy Mickelsen. She is my better half. I was born and raised in Utah, on a farm. Learned how to be a hardworker from a young age. I graduated from the University of Utah in Economics. My love for learning has brought me into a career of web development for the purpose that it is an ever evolving career and you can always learn some new way to make web applications and this is amazing!
